About Healthhub

Exploring the Wonders of the Brain Starts Here

Welcome to HealthHub, a revolutionary web app crafted by our team of AI Brainiacs during a dynamic hackathon! Designed with African medical students in mind, HealthHub empowers you to generate lifelike brain MRIs, identify tumors, do 3D visualisations , explore the complexities of the brain, and engage with our interactive chatbot. Take your knowledge to new heights with our quiz generator. Join us on a journey of discovery and learning as we unravel the mysteries of the brain together!
